Privacy Policy

Your trust matters. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring your personal information is secure.

We may collect basic details such as your name, contact information, delivery address, and payment method. We only request information necessary to process your orders and improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

How We Protect Your Data

All personal information is handled responsibly and securely. We use standard safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, and we do not share or sell your data to external parties except as required to fulfill your order or comply with the law.

Policy Updates
